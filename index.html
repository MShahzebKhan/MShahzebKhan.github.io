Bio

I am Muhammad Shahzeb Khan, a Ph.D. graduate in Informatics from FAU Erlangen-Nürnberg where my research focused on the neural network-based processing of light fields. I also hold an MS degree in Computer Science from Istanbul Medipol University, where I specialized in super-resolution of light fields using convolutional neural networks. Additionally, I have a BE degree in Electronics from PAF-KIET in Karachi. With over 5 years of experience in programming and deep learning, I have worked extensively with Python and Matlab. I am proficient in TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Keras for deep learning applications and have experience with various programming libraries such as OpenCV, Numpy, and Pandas. Throughout my career, I have contributed to research projects, developed algorithms, and conducted experiments in prestigious institutions such as Fraunhofer IIS and Istanbul Medipol University. Please explore the page to learn more about my publications and professional experiences.
